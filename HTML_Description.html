Student B. R. was annoyed by TV commercials. He suspected that there were more commercials in the "basic" TV channels, the ones that come with a cable TV subscription, than in the "extended" channels you pay extra for. To check this, he collected the data shown in Table 1.

He measured an average of 9.21 minutes of commercials per half hour in the basic channels, vs only 6.87 minutes in the extended channels. This seems to support his hypothesis. But there is not much data perhaps the difference was just random. The poor guy could only stand to watch 20 random half hours of TV.
